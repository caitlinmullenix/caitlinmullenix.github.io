I was presented with the challenege to recreate a Chuck Close portrait of myself. Chuck Close is known for creating portraits of people by using a grid method. By using shapes and colors, Close portrays portraits in a way that no one sees a face! This piece required some colored pencils and a lot of patience
2 / 5

For one of my final courses at CU Boulder, we were assigned to create a new brand and design a product with it. For my project, I decided to create a tequila brand and called it "CLM Tequila". I named it CLM because my name is Caitlin and my sisters names are Lauren and Morgan. Growing up “we were the 3 best friends that anyone could have” but that drove my parents insane. We were monsters. But, now that we are all adults, we look back on our monster days and drink about it while laughing and remincising. With the help of this tequila, my sisters and I are hoping to help all of you bring back your inner child. This project required Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD, and Adobe Illustrator.
