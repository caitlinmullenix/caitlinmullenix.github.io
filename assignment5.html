Challenge 6: Exploring New Tags or Attributes

For this portion of the challenge, I added a new tag that allowed me to play my favorite YouTube video on my About Me page (assignment 1).

I wanted my page to show my favorite video on YouTube because of the colors and asethic Brodie Moss always has in his videos. His videos are so relaxing to watch and I wanted to be able to share that through my page.

I googled how to play a YouTube video in HTML and found this website that explained how to do it.

iFrame is the element that allowed me to play the video. Once I added that element, I had to set the width and height of the video to make it look normal on my page. After that, I had to source the video in Atom by finding the code for the video which I found under the share button in Youtube, then I pasted it within the src option. Then I closed the element iFrame.
